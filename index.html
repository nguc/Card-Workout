Card Workout

How the workout works

The suit and the number of each card determine the exercise you will perform and the number of reps.
If your card is a SPADE, you will be performing a PUSH exercise
If your card is a CLUB, you will be performing a PULL exercise
If your card is a HEART, you will be performing a LEGS exercise
If your card is a DIAMOND, you will be performing an ISOLATION exercise
To determine how many reps you will perform and the rest you may take between sets, follow these guidelines:
• The number of reps you perform is the card number, PLUS 10
• If you have a face card (jack, queen and king), you must perform 20 reps
• If you have an ace card, take 2 minutes of rest (NOTE: Do not take rest between card draws, unless you turn over an ace)

Ace - Rest 2 minutes!

Spades - Push

  1. Push up or incline pushups
  2. Pike push ups or vertical pushups
  3. Standing dumbbell press
  4. Lateral raises or timed doorway later holds (eg. 16 reps = 16 sec)

Clubs - Pull

  1. Pull ups
  2. Inverted row or dumbbell row
  3. Rear delt flyes
  4. Upright row

Hearts - Legs

  1. Walking lunges
  2. Bulgarian split squat
  3. Single leg hip thrust
  4. Nordic ham curl

Diamonds - Isolation

  1. Biceps: weighted curl or weightless flex curls
  2. Triceps: Single arm side push up or skullcrushers
  3. Abs: Bicycle crunch or reverse crunch
  4. Calves: Standing calf raise
